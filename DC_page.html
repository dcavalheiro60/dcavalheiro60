About Me

Additionally, I am a Board of Directors Member and Administrator for a Prominent School in São Paulo, an Advisory Board member for Hotelaria Brasil Group-Barão Geraldo Branch, and the Founder of a business consulting firm specializing in long-term and short-term contracts.

Key services provided including:

  • Maintain internal safeguards for revenue receipts, costs, team and organizational budgets and expenditures.
  • Finance forecasting. Preparation of monthly financial reports and cash flow management.
  • Provision of comprehensive financial reporting and updates to senior managers.
  • Evaluation and analysis of all operational and investment strategies.
  • Establishment of financial and operational benchmarks, budgets, project monitoring, and reporting standards in coordination with upper management on a biweekly, monthly, and annual basis.
  • Implementation of consistent accounting policies, practices, procedures and controls across all divisions, ensuring compliance with legal standards regulations while staying informed on proposed legislation.
  • Redefined job responsibilities and functions for administrative and operational staff, resulting in notable increases in job performance, task effectiveness, job satisfaction, and productivity.
